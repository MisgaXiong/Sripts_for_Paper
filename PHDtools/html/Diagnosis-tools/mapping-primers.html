Strain-level mapping primers
Identify the mismatches of primers against to each strain of a pathogen.
Pathogen genomes

The strain-level primers mapping can provide the useful information to evaluate the conservation of primers against to the pathogen. The name of primers/probe must be "forward", "reverse" or "probe". And the completed or nearly-full length genomes of each isolate of the certain pathogen must be in a fasta file.
